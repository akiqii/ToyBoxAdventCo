Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: When will my calendar arrive?

A: Orders are processed within 2–3 business days. For delivery, please allow around 7 days within Asia and up to 14 days worldwide. To receive your calendar before December 1st, please order by 15 November; for Christmas Day delivery, please order by 1 December.

Q: What age range are the toys suitable for?

A: Most of our calendars are designed for children aged 3 and up. Please check the specific product description for age recommendations and small parts warnings.

Q: Can I return a calendar if my child already has it?

A: Yes, we accept returns on unopened calendars within 30 days of purchase. Please see the Shipping Info / Returns page for full details.

Shipping & Delivery Information

Shipping Rates & Times

  • Asia: Estimated delivery within 7 days after dispatch. Shipping is free.
  • Worldwide: Estimated delivery within 14 days after dispatch. Flat shipping fee of $10 per order.
  • All orders are processed within 2–3 business days. Tracking information will be sent via email once your order ships.
  • For delivery before 1 December, please place your order by 15 November. For delivery by Christmas Day, please order by 1 December.

Returns Policy

We want you to be delighted with your purchase. If you need to make a return, please note the following:

  • Return window: You may request a return within 7 days of receiving your order.
  • Condition of items: Items should be unused and in their original packaging unless they arrived damaged or defective.
  • Damaged or incorrect items: If your calendar arrives damaged or incorrect, we will cover the return shipping cost.
  • Change of mind: For other reasons (e.g. change of mind), a return shipping fee may apply.
  • To initiate a return, please contact us via the Contact Us page with your order number and details.
